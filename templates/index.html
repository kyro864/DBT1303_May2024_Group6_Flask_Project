Welcome To Climate Change Conference.

Join the fight against climate change

Climate change is one of the most pressing issues of our time, and we are dedicated to driving progress and taking action to protect our planet. We invite you to be a part of this crucial movement.

Our upcoming conference will bring together experts, activists, policymakers, and concerned citizens from around the globe to address the challenges posed by climate change and explore sustainable solutions. With a range of events, including keynote speeches, panel discussions, workshops, and networking opportunities, this conference promises to be a hub of innovation, collaboration, and impactful ideas.

Conference Details:

Dates: 17-18 August, 2024

Location: KICC, Nairobi, Kenya

Theme: Water For Life

Why Attend?

  • Impactful Discussions: Engage in thought-provoking conversations and gain insights from diverse perspectives.
  • Knowledge Sharing: Learn from leading experts and stay updated on the latest research and advancements in climate change mitigation and adaptation.
  • Networking Opportunities: Connect with like-minded individuals and organizations, fostering collaborations and partnerships.
